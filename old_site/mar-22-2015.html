Officer Elections Information

Posted on March 22, 2015 by SCR Webmaster

When: Wednesday March 25, 2014

When: Carson 121

Time: 6-7:30pm

Available Positions: President, Vice-President, Secretary, Treasurer

Officer Responsibilities and Eligibility Requirements:

President

Responsibilities
  • Provide the vision and direction for the organization.
  • Preside over all Leadership Team meetings.
  • Delegate tasks to the rest of the Leadership Team as needed.
  • Approve the distribution of SCR funds to the SCR teams.
  • Oversee interactions with corporate sponsors.
  • Oversee changes to the Constitution and By-Laws.
Eligibility Requirements
  • Be a student in good standing at the University of Oklahoma.
  • Have completed at least two (2) years of regular involvement with SCR.
  • Have previously served on the Leadership Team.

Vice-President

Responsibilities
  • Serve as Acting President in the event that the President cannot fulfill his or her duties.
  • Assist the President as needed.
  • Assume the role of President should the current President vacate his or her duties.
  • Oversee the publicity and advertising of SCR and its events.
  • Oversee the construction and use of outreach-oriented demonstrations.
Eligibility Requirements
  • Be a student in good standing at the University of Oklahoma.
  • Have completed at least one (1) year of regular involvement with SCR.

Secretary

Responsibilities
  • Oversee and manage the documentation and organization of the SCR Leadership Team.
  • Assist the President as needed.
  • Assume the role of President should the current President and Vice President both vacate their respective roles.
  • Oversee the completion and submission of any forms, registrations, or applications related to SCR as an Organization. Examples include annual re-registration, application for funding, and licensing approval for new designs.
  • Oversee the organization of all technical records and databases shared by SCR and its teams.
Eligibility Requirements
  • Be a student in good standing at the University of Oklahoma.
  • Have completed at least one (1) year of regular involvement with SCR.

Treasurer

Responsibilities
  • Oversee and manage any and all funding held or spent by SCR or its teams.
  • Assist the President as needed.
  • Assume the role of President should the current President, Vice President, and Secretary all vacate their respective roles.
  • Know the amount of money in all SCR accounts at all times.
  • Collect and maintain Spending Report Forms.
  • Submit receipts for reimbursement.
  • Monitor and classify the funds spent by each SCR team.
Eligibility Requirements
  • Be a student in good standing at the University of Oklahoma.
  • Have completed at least one (1) year of regular involvement with SCR.
