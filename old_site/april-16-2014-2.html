SCR Tech Adviser Positions Open

Posted on April 16, 2014 by SCR Webmaster

Sooner Competitive Robotics is now accepting applications for the Tech Adviser position!

A Technical Adviser is an officer-level position with a focus on technical operations. This stands in contrast to the officers, who focus on organizational operations. There may be as many technical advisers as there are officers. Technical Advisers may serve multiple terms, but they must be re-elected each term.

Responsibilities of Tech Adviser
  • Generally be available for consultation via email, phone, and in-person appointments;
  • Serve as a member of the Leadership Team, performing the duties thereof; and
  • Advise the ASEE underclassmen team in designing and building their robot.
  • Actively watch for ways to improve the designs of all SCR teams;
  • Be familiar with past designs used in SCR and their outcomes;
  • Serve as an active member of at least one SCR team; and
  • Advise each team with a friendly and non-judgmental demeanor.

Eligibility Requirements

Note: These requirements will be relaxed since this is the first year we are accepting applications. A strong technical background with another competition team will be sufficient.

  • Be a student in good standing at the University of Oklahoma;
  • Have served as a Team Captain or technical Sub-Team Lead in SCR;
  • Have served at least one (1) year in SCR, with a preference given to those with two (2) or more years of experience;
  • Currently hold no position as an Officer or Team Captain within SCR;
  • Be nominated by the Leadership Team and approve that nomination, or submit an application of intent to the Leadership Team; and
  • Be approved by a majority of the current (i.e. outgoing) SCR Leadership Team after Officer and Captain elections.

Additionally, the Technical Advisers should represent diverse technical backgrounds.

Mechanical, Electrical/Computer, and Computer Science Majors with experience are strongly encouraged to apply!

Applications should be sent to scr@ou.edu by April 23rd, 2014 at 11:59 pm.
